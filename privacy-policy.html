Section 01

Overview & Who We Are

Welcome to Trade Counter Picks ("we," "us," or "our"). We operate a curated product reference website focused on helping home builders and renovation contractors across the United States find reliable, code-compliant plumbing, fixture, and trade-grade hardware from established suppliers.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect any information when you visit our website hosted on GitHub Pages. We are committed to being transparent about our practices and respecting your privacy at every step.

🔑 Short version: We run a simple, informational website with affiliate links to supplier and retail partners. We do not collect personal data. We do not run ads, sell data, or create user accounts.

Section 02

Information We Collect

Because Trade Counter Picks is a static informational website with no registration, login, or forms, we collect very limited information:

  • Automatically collected data: When you visit our site, standard web server logs may record your IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and time of visit. This data is handled by GitHub Pages (our hosting provider) and is subject to GitHub's Privacy Statement. We do not independently access or store these logs.
  • Affiliate click data: When you click a product link on our site, our affiliate network partner (Impact, or other listed partners) and the destination retailer may set cookies on your browser to track purchases. This data is collected and controlled by those third parties, not by us. See Section 5 for full details.

ℹ️ We do not use contact forms, newsletter sign-ups, or any data entry fields on our website. We do not collect any personal information from visitors.

Section 03

How We Use Your Information

We use any information we receive solely for the following purposes:

  • To understand general traffic patterns on our site so we can improve the content we offer (using anonymized, aggregated data only).
  • To maintain and operate our website in accordance with GitHub Pages hosting requirements.
  • To fulfill our obligations under our affiliate partner agreements, including ensuring our links are correctly formatted and our disclosures are visible.

We do not use your information to send you unsolicited emails, serve you advertisements, build behavioral profiles, or sell to third-party data brokers.

Section 04

Cookies & Tracking

Our own website does not place any first-party cookies or tracking scripts on your device. However, when you click a product link on our site, the destination retailer or our affiliate network (such as Impact) will use their own cookies and tracking mechanisms to identify purchases that originated from our referral links.

These affiliate tracking cookies typically have a limited duration window after your click. If you complete a qualifying purchase within that window, we may receive a small commission at no additional cost to you.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Doing so will not prevent you from visiting our site or viewing our content, though it may affect how retailer cart and checkout functions work.

ℹ️ For more information on how to control cookies, visit AllAboutCookies.org, a free consumer resource.

🛠️

Affiliate Program Disclosure

Trade Counter Picks participates in affiliate marketing programs, including the Impact affiliate network and partner brand programs (such as Ferguson).

This means that when you click a product link on our site and make a qualifying purchase, we may earn a commission. The price you pay is exactly the same — our commission comes from the retailer's existing margin, not from any markup charged to you.

Our product recommendations are based entirely on research, code compliance, brand reliability, and suitability for US residential construction and renovation projects. We only feature products we genuinely believe are a strong fit for builders and contractors. Affiliate commissions do not influence which products we feature or how we describe them.

Section 06

Third-Party Links & Services

Our website contains links to supplier and retail websites and may reference other external resources. Once you leave our site, you are subject to the privacy policies of those third-party websites. We are not responsible for the content, security, or privacy practices of any external sites.

Key third-party services that may be involved when you use our site:

  • Impact: Our affiliate links may be tracked through the Impact affiliate network. See the Impact Privacy Policy for details.
  • Ferguson and other retail partners: Product links lead to partner retailer websites. Each retailer's data practices are governed by their own privacy policy, linked on their respective websites.
  • GitHub Pages: Our site is hosted on GitHub Pages, a service of GitHub Inc. GitHub may collect server log data as described in the GitHub Privacy Statement.
  • Google Fonts: Our site uses Google Fonts for typography. Google may log font requests. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.
Section 07

Children's Privacy

Trade Counter Picks is intended for adults, including contractors, builders, and trade professionals managing construction and renovation projects. Our website is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our site has no forms or data entry fields, and we do not collect such information from any visitor.

Section 08

Data Security

Since our website is a static HTML site with no database, forms, or user account system, we hold no personal data about our visitors.

Our site is served over HTTPS via GitHub Pages, which means all data transmitted between your browser and our site is encrypted in transit. We take reasonable precautions to protect any information involved in our operations, though no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Section 09

Your Rights & Choices

As a visitor to our site, you have the following rights with respect to your personal information:

  • Cookie Control: You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.
  • Opt-Out of Retailer Tracking: Most retail partners, including Amazon and Ferguson, offer interest-based advertising opt-outs directly through their own account or privacy settings.
  • Do Not Track: Our static website does not run tracking scripts, so no additional Do Not Track (DNT) configuration is needed on our end.

ℹ️ California residents: Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have the right to know, delete, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not collect or sell personal data.

ℹ️ Virginia residents: Under the Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA), you have rights to access, correct, delete, and opt out of targeted advertising. We do not conduct targeted advertising or collect personal data.

Changes to This Policy

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of our website after any changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.
